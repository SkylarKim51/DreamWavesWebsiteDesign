Welcome to DreamWaves

DreamWaves is a student run music club at UCR, created in the spring of 2019. Our goal is to provide local, up and coming artists with a platform to share their music and performances.

In the spring of 2021, DreamWaves will host a small group of concerts at the UCR campus. The performances will be entirely made up of college students whether they are pursuing a music career or just enjoy making music as a hobby. DreamWaves will not be limited to any number of genres, and each artist will be given ample time to showcase their music.
