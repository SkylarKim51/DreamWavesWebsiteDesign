Covid-19

Due to the current Covid-19 situation, all proceedings of DreamWaves have been halted.
Unfortunately, the pandemic hit during the preliminary building of the DreamWaves club.
Many club members have had to move home during these unprecedented times in order to stay safe
or save money. Looking to the future, it seems that all members of the club will have already
graduated and moved away by the time any safe proceedings can be made. It is with great sorrow
that our goal of providing young artists a platform will not be achieved with DreamWaves at this time.
We thank you for your understanding.
